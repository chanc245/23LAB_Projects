Web Based Reading Response

I truly believe that there are endless possibilities when it comes to creating a website.
As an artist who wants to investigate website interactivity,
I want to create a website that incorporates interaction.

In a previous project, I had designed a website with a similar function,
but encountered an issue where the images would stick together.
This time, I have successfully resolved that problem.

Given the author's mention of a house, a cloud, and later, a rock,
my goal is to allow viewers to freely drag
around these elements and engage with them.
